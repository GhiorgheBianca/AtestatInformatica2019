In part two of three of the series "Through the Looking Glass", we cover the middle of the company's lifespan, with the classic cyberpunk horror hit System Shock, the self-publishing years which saw the release of the Flight Unlimited games, Terra Nova: Strike Force Centauri and British Open Championship Golf. Also covered is the legendary title Thief: The Dark Project, its evolution from Dark Camelot, and its inspirational features and themes.
